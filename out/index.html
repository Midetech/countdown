A word from our CEO

Dear Customers,

I’m thrilled to announce that today Ugabus has been acquired by Treepz Global, a leading vehicle-hailing app that offers commuters comfort, safety and convenience.

What does this mean for you?

On a day-to-day, it means better bus ticketing experience, reliable travel and a smoother customer experience. This service will now be known as travel treepz. It is important to know that you will continue to enjoy the Ugabus experience, but in an improved way. On top of inter-town bus tickets, TREEPZ Uganda will also offer a Cooperate travel service which will allow companies partner with us for bus hires and staff pick-ups/drop-offs. Additionally, in the near future, we will be introducing more services including daily treepz which would see our customers enjoy a daily commuter experience in and around the city.

What does this mean for us?

I will continue to lead this dedicated and talented team under Treepz.com, but our entire staff is now a part of the Treepz family! Our head office will now be Toronto, Canada with Kampala becoming an operations centre for Treepz Global.

Our story continues…

5 years ago, we set out with a bold mission to digitize bus operations in Uganda and make bus travel extremely convenient for everyone. By the end of 2017, I had personally held a conversation with 97% touts, 87% bus owners, 100% booking clerks and knocked on so many doors of policy makers. Even when some kicked us out of their offices, it was our persistence that later opened those doors. It was the positive feedback from you our dear customers that kept us going. It was that bus ticket you booked through the Ugabus app that kept our doors open and our minds working to keep serving you better. We believed that the bus industry deserved a loyal and reliable operator to deliver value above self. This is also Treepz.com mission. Joining forces with a company as passionate about shared mobility as us, is what takes us into the next chapter in our storybook.

Stay tuned to experience the most robust shared mobility happening right at your fingertips.

You can read the TREEPZ GLOBAL press release here and if you have any further questions, please reach out to us and we will be more than happy to provide clarity.

Sincerest gratitude,

I look forward to exciting stuff with Treepz Uganda.

Hakiza Ronald

CEO Ugabus Now TREEPZ
